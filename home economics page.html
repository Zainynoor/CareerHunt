HOME ECONOMICS

INTRODUCTION

Home economics,domestic science or home science is a field of study that deals with the
relationship between individuals, families,communities,and the environment in which they live.

The study of the care and management of a household.

Branches of Home Economics

  • Cooking(Since food preparation was central to homemaking.)
  • Child Development
  • Education and Community Awareness
  • Home management and Design
  • Sewing and Textiles
  • Budgeting and Economics
  • Health and Hygiene
