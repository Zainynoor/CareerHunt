BIOLOGY

INTRODUCTION

  • Biology is the branch of science concerned with the study of life and living organisms,including their structure,growth,function,evolution,reproduction and taxonomy.
  • Biology generally recognizes the cell as the basic unit of life,genes as the basic unit of heredity and evolution as the engine that propels the synthesis and creation of new species.
  • The term Biology derived from the greek word bios,"life" and the suffix-logia,"study of".

Types of Biology

Biology is classified in 3 main types:

Zoology is the study of behaviour,
structure,physiology and distribution
of animals.

Botany is the study of genetics, ecology,
structure,physiology and distribution
and economic importance of plants.

Microbiology is the study of
microscopic organisms
like viruses,bacteria,algea,fungi,
slime molds and protozoa.
