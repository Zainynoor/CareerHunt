Matric is the first level where you can select subject groups of your choice.

There are basically two options for you to select in matric:

  1. SCIENCE(Biology,Computer)
  2. ARTS
  • Compulsory subjects:
    1. English
    2. Urdu
    3. Pakistan studies
    4. Islamic studies(for muslims)
    5. Ethics studies(for non-muslims)

SCIENCE GROUP(BIO)

In science group BIO,we study following subjects:

  1. Biology
  2. Physics
  3. Chemistry
  4. Maths

SCIENCE GROUP(COMPUTER)

In science group COMPUTER,we study following subjects:

  1. Computer science
  2. Physics
  3. Chemistry
  4. Maths

ARTS GROUP

In Arts group,we further have 2 options:

  1. Computer Arts
  2. Simple Arts

In COMPUTER ARTS,we have elective subjects:

  • Computer
  • General Science
  • General Maths
  • Home Economics/Civics

In SIMPLE ARTS,we have elective subjects:

  • Islamiat Elective
  • General Science
  • General Maths
  • Home Economics/Civics
